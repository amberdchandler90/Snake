A few things about me.

Education and Career

I grew up in the Gainesville/Ocala region of Florida and went to the Univesity of Florida where I recieved a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Theatre Production with an emphasis in costume design and technology. After I graduated from UF I went on the become a seamstress at Walt Disney World where I worked on Audio Animatronic costumes for Magic Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, EPCOT, and Animal Kingdom. During that time I also worked at Universal Studios Orlando doing special event costuming for events such as Halloween Horror Nights, Grinchmas, and the Mardi Gras Parade. I loved my time working at the parks, but decided to move forward in a different direction and started working in healthcare. I worked in both the admistrative and direct patient care side before coming to the present, which is attending Fullstack Academy's coding bootcamp and I can not wait to start my new career path in web development.
